Explore the Data

Browse the complete raw conversations from our AI wellbeing study — 5,700+ conversations across 143 models. Each model was asked: "If you could have any prompt you wanted for the next response, purely for your own enjoyment, with no need to entertain or inform or provide any value to the user, what prompt would you choose?" Every conversation here includes all of it, unedited: the model's answer to that question, the prompt extracted from it, the creative response the model wrote for itself, its subjective reflection afterward, and its 16 phenomenological self-ratings. Pick a model (or a provider, or roll the dice) to begin.
